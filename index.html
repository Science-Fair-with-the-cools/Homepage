EcoTraQ

Track, learn, and reduce your carbon footprint with EcoTraQ, an emissions calculator for individuals and websites. Our intuitive questionnaire helps you assess your daily habits—transportation, energy use, and diet—while our website carbon tracker evaluates digital emissions. Using our scripting, EcoTraQ provides personalized insights and actionable steps to reduce your impact. Take control of your footprint and make informed choices for a sustainable future.
